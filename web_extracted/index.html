Under the Hood

Geometry Engine
Does the Heavy Work

Harfex computes wall offsets, boolean operations and mesh generation automatically. You focus on design — the engine handles manufacturing geometry.

1

Vector Parsing

DXF/SVG paths are read, cleaned and normalized for 3D processing.

2

Wall & Face Computation

Shapely + Manifold3D computes precise offsets and boolean operations.

3

Mesh Output

Watertight STL/3MF meshes ready for direct 3D printer slicing.
